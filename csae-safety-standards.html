ZipMate Safety Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation

Our Commitment: ZipMate is committed to creating a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content or behavior on our platform.

Age Requirements

ZipMate is strictly for users aged 18 and above. We take age verification seriously to protect minors and maintain a safe community.

  • Minimum Age: Users must be 18 years or older to use ZipMate
  • Age Verification: We verify age during registration through email verification and may require additional verification if needed
  • Underage Detection: Users found to be underage will have their accounts immediately terminated
  • Monitoring: We use automated systems and manual review to detect and remove underage accounts
  • Reporting: Users can report suspected underage accounts through our in-app reporting system

Content Moderation

We employ multiple layers of content moderation to ensure a safe environment:

  1. Automated Systems: AI-powered content scanning for inappropriate content, including CSAE material
  2. User Reporting: Easy-to-use reporting tools throughout the app on profiles, messages, and photos
  3. Human Review: Trained moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours, with urgent cases escalated immediately
  4. Photo Verification: Required verification process for profile photos to ensure authenticity
  5. Proactive Monitoring: Continuous monitoring of user-generated content and behavior patterns

Reporting CSAE Content

If you encounter content or behavior that violates our CSAE policy, please report it immediately:

Reporting Methods:

  • In-App Reporting: Use the "Report" button on any profile, message, or photo
  • Email: safety@zipmate.app
  • Emergency: Contact local law enforcement immediately if you believe a child is in immediate danger

Response Time: All reports are reviewed within 24 hours, with urgent cases escalated immediately to our safety team and law enforcement when necessary.

Our Response to Violations

When CSAE content or behavior is detected or reported:

  • Immediate Removal: All CSAE content is removed immediately upon detection
  • Account Termination: Permanent account termination for offenders, with no opportunity for appeal
  • Law Enforcement: Immediate cooperation with law enforcement investigations
  • Evidence Preservation: Preservation of relevant data as required by law for investigations
  • Platform Bans: Device and IP-based bans to prevent repeat offenders

User Safety Resources

We provide resources to help users stay safe and report concerns:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC): www.missingkids.org
  • CyberTipline: report.cybertip.org
  • Local Law Enforcement: Contact your local police department for immediate threats
  • Victim Support: Resources available through NCMEC and local victim support organizations

Law Enforcement Cooperation

ZipMate fully cooperates with law enforcement agencies investigating CSAE cases. Our commitment includes:

  • Data Preservation: Preserving relevant data as required by law and legal requests
  • Prompt Response: Responding promptly to valid legal requests and subpoenas
  • International Cooperation: Working with international law enforcement agencies
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with all applicable laws and regulations, including:
    • PROTECT Act (United States)
    • EU Directive on combating child sexual abuse
    • Local laws in all countries where ZipMate operates

Prevention and Education

We believe prevention is key to protecting children:

  • User Education: Safety tips and guidelines provided to all users
  • Community Guidelines: Clear rules about acceptable behavior and content
  • Regular Training: Ongoing training for our moderation team on identifying and handling CSAE content
  • Technology Updates: Continuous improvement of our detection and prevention systems

Policy Updates

This policy is reviewed regularly and updated as needed to reflect:

  • Changes in laws and regulations
  • Improvements in detection technology
  • Feedback from users and safety organizations
  • Best practices in the industry
